Discover Mabini

Adventure by the Sea, Peace by the Shore

Welcome to Mabini

Mabini, Batangas is a beautiful coastal town with clear beaches, amazing dive spots, and scenic views of the sea and mountains. Whether you want an underwater adventure or a relaxing getaway by the shore, Mabini has something for everyone.

Top Destinations

Anilao Diving Spots

Experience one of the Philippines' premier dive sites with colorful coral reefs and rich marine life.

Batangas Beaches

Relax on pristine beaches and enjoy the calm waves, perfect for family getaways.

Mt. Gulugod Baboy

Take a scenic hike with panoramic views of Mabini Batangas.
